Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you connect a social media account, we collect:

  • Your platform user ID and display name
  • An access token to read your recent posts
  • The ID of your most recent post (to detect new ones)

2. How We Use Your Data

Data is used solely to detect when you publish new content and send a notification to a Discord server you have configured. We do not analyze, sell, or share your data.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored in a secured database. Access tokens are stored to allow periodic checks and are not used for any other purpose.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained until you disconnect your account. Upon disconnection, all associated data is deleted.

5. Third Parties

We use the following third-party services:

  • TikTok API – to read your video list
  • Instagram Graph API – to read your media list
  • Discord – to send notifications
  • Neon (PostgreSQL) – for data storage

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or revoking access in your platform's app settings.
